Roasted brussels sprouts

Ingredients

  • cut brussels sprouts in half
  • Garlic salt to taste

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 425oF.
  2. Chop up the brussels into bite-sides pieces.
  3. Spread the brussles all over the pan with olive oil. Also sprinkle olive oil all over them.
  4. Mix together the olive oil, brussles, and add in additional salt top the pan.
  5. Cook to brussles for 13-15 minutes in the oven until cooked to your taste.
  6. Then take out of oven and enjoy.
