Dance Moms

Produced in Abby Lee Miller's dance studio

The show Dance Mom's is about a group of girls who all dance for Abby Lee Miller. Abby Lee Miller is a super strict dance teacher who is known for using intimidating tactics to coach the young girls

Over time, the moms slowly leave Abby due to her bad treatment of the girls, however, Abby did manage to make all these girls famous from the show.
